FOOL'S GOLD CHARACTER DECK BUILDER

Build your Character Deck

In many RPGs players interact with the game by rolling dice to decide the outcome of their characters’ actions. In Fool’s Gold instead of dice you use a deck of custom cards called the Character Deck. This deck is more than just a randomiser to determine success or failure; it is a way to visually represent your character’s strengths and weaknesses. You can use XP to upgrade or get new skills or you can upgrade your Character Deck by swapping in and out different kinds of cards.

To make a first level character deck you get 32 points to buy cards. Before spending any points to buy cards a first level character deck starts with the following cards:

  • 3 Basic Mind Cards
  • 3 Basic Body Cards
  • 3 Basic Face Cards
  • 3 Failure Cards
  • 2 Partial Success Cards
  • 10 Bronze Success Cards
  • 1 Silver Success Card

To these initial 25 cards you must add an extra 15 cards so that your Character Deck has 40 cards.

Your deck can’t have more than 40 cards except in special situations such as when the GM gives you Boons and Curses. See page ? for more details.

The card types, their cost, and the cap (the limit to how many you can first add to your deck) are listed below:

Card Type Point Cost Cap
Basic Attribute 2 points 8 cards of any type, 24 in total
Dual Attribute 4 points 4 cards of any type, 6 in total
Triple Attribute 6 points 2 cards in total
Partial Success 0 points 8 cards in total
Bronze Success 2 points 16 cards in total
Silver Success 4 points 3 cards in total
Gold Success* 8 points 1 card in total
Failure 0 points 6 cards in total

*Gold Success cards are not available during character creation and can only be bought by using XP with the exception being one shot games when the GM and players want to do a high powered game with very strong characters.
